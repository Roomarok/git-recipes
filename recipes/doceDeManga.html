Doce de Manga!

My preferred mango sweet

The perfect recipe, can be made anywhere anytime any day. Lovely to eat while studying to give that extra energy

A way to make everyone that tastes it fall in love with you. Recipe made by Dalton. Posted too on this site.

Ingredients

For this recipe you'll need the following ingredients:

Main Mixture

  • Mangos (3 Und.)
  • Sugar (3 Cups)
  • Unflavored Gelatin (1 Packt.)

Instructions for the recipe

  1. Peel the mangos and chop them into small cubes
  2. Put the sugar and the chopped mangos inside a Pressure Pan. Turn on the heat, mix it until the sugar melts the close it and leave in medium heat for 4 minutes then turn off the heat.
  3. Carefully when opening the pan, make sure there is not much pressure left. You will notice that the mixture is very jellowy, that's the point of it.
  4. Put the contents off the unflavored gelatin pac in the mixture, mix it well and let it cool down in the fridge.
  5. In my case i like to leave it for 24 hour inside before eating, but it's all personal.
