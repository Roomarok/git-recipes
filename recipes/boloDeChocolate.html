Bolo de Chocolate!!

the best chocolate cake?

It's an amazing recipe do make on weekends, perfect for you to enjoy alone with some nice tea or to present your family at your next visit home.

A lovely way to make everyone around you have a enjoyable evening or morning. Recipe made by Sandro Valério, It's his go to for chocolate cake. posted too on this site.

Ingredients

For this recipe you'll need the following ingredients:

Main Mixture

  • All-purpose flour (3 Cups)
  • Sugar (2 Cups)
  • Milk (1 Cup)
  • Chocolate Powder (4 Cups)
  • Eggs (4 Und)
  • Butter (2 Table Spoons)
  • Baking Powder(2 Table Spoons)

"Frosting"

  • Butter (2 Table Spoons)
  • Chocolate Powder (7 Table Spoons)
  • Milk Cream (2 Cans)
  • Sugar (3 Table Spoons)

Instructions for the recipe

  1. Pre heat the oven to 180°C
  2. In a mixer add almost all(except the baking powder) the Main Mixture ingredients(all-purpose flour, butter, etc) and mixer for around 5 minutes. Until you get a homogenous mixture
  3. Put the missing ingredient, the baking powder, with a spoon and gently mix it all up
  4. Add the mixture in a butter greased cake pan and put it in the pre-heated oven for 40 minutes. Keep in mind that it will grow quite a lot, so don't forget to use a large enough cake pan so that it doesn't spill out
  5. Now that we can work on the frosting, with the milk cream in hand, add it to nice pot that you can mix it until it achieves a consistent texture
  6. In a pan melt the butter and add the frosting portion of the chocolate powder and mix it, after it's homogenous turn off the heat add the sugar then drop it inside the pot with the milk cream
  7. Drop the frosting on top of the cake and cover all the missing spots and you're done!!
